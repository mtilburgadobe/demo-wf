3C. "mortgage" Template Has 89 Pages (Only 13 /mortgage/* URLs Exist)
89 vs 13
Mortgage Page Count

Issue: The HTML inventory assigns 89 pages to the mortgage template, but the sitemap only has 13 URLs under /mortgage/*.

Hypothesis: The HTML likely groups additional product pages (some /checking/*, /auto-loans/*, /savings-cds/* sub-pages) under a broader "mortgage" template name — possibly all pages that share a product-detail layout with rate tables and fee disclosures.

Fix needed: Clarify which 89 URLs map to the HTML's "mortgage" template. This may require a different template assignment strategy in our CSV.

3D. "investing" Template Has 21 Pages (Only 1 /investing-wealth/ URL Exists)
21 vs 1
Investing Page Count

Issue: The HTML inventory assigns 21 pages to investing, but the sitemap has only 1 URL at /investing-wealth/.

Hypothesis: The HTML likely includes /goals-investing/*, /premier/, and possibly some /about/investor-relations/* pages under this template.

Our CSV: Maps those URLs to T5 Education Article, T3 Product Detail, and T5 Education Article respectively.

Fix needed: Identify the full URL list for HTML's "investing" template scope.
